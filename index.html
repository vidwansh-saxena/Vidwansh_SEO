FAQ

Common Questions

How long does SEO take to show results?+

SEO is a long-term investment. You typically start seeing meaningful movement in 3-6 months for competitive keywords, with significant results at 6-12 months. Local SEO and low-competition keywords can show results faster — often within 4-8 weeks.

What SEO services do you offer?+

I offer a complete SEO suite: Link Building, On-Page SEO, Technical SEO, Local SEO (Google Business Profile), Keyword Research, Content Strategy, and full SEO Audits. My 3-person team handles everything under one roof.

How much does SEO cost?+

Pricing depends on the scope of work, competition level, and your target market. I offer flexible packages for small businesses, startups and established companies. Contact me for a custom quote after your free audit.

Do you offer a free SEO audit?+

Yes! I offer a complimentary SEO audit where I'll analyse your website, identify key issues, and outline quick wins. No cost, no obligation — just fill out the contact form below.

Do you work with US and UK clients?+

Absolutely. I work with clients across India, the US, and UK fully remotely. I'm comfortable with different time zones and deliver transparent weekly reports so you're always in the loop regardless of your location.
